"Echoes of Eternia" describes a vivid and fantastical landscape characterized by elements of crystal, shell, and sleek white armor. The setting features an intricate 144-core pattern that enhances every sensory experience to hyper-realistic levels. Greenery adds life and vibrancy, permeating the scene with an embracing presence. At its heart is a magnificent city made entirely of crystal, which stretches upwards towards the sky, exuding beauty. Surrounding this urban marvel are hills defined by the McGovern-DePablo boundary, emphasizing the expansive landscape.
